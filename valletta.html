Malta, Valletta

For such a tiny place – it’s the smallest capital in Europe – Valletta has a lot to shout about. There are more than 350 historical monuments within a third of a square mile, so it’s not surprising that UNESCO, when it named the walled city a World Heritage Site, described it as “one of the most concentrated historic areas in the world”.
